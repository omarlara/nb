Moving Tips

Moving can be a busy time. Here a few tips that you may find helpful:

  • Plan and prepare. Dedicate some time to think through what living at your new place will be like. If you're moving into your very fist home, a new climate or perhaps from an apartment to house, you may need to purchase some new items.
  • Plan and compare. It's a good idea to request an estimate of monthly utility bills from the seller or previous tenant so that you're prepared and have an idea of what to expect.
  • Purge and pack. Think of your move as an opportunity to sell or donate items you no longer. This will lighten your load and simplify packing. Start packing with infrequently used items, such as offseason decorations and the waffle iron.
  • Label and list. INvest the time to take inventory of what you put where. Mark the destination roms as well and label boxes you'll need to open right away with 'essentials' to help make the unpacking effort as smooth as possible.
